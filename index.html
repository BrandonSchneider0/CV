Brandon Schneider

Musician, Performer

Brandon Schneider is an Orlando based performer, and started in 2019 with the Universal Holiday parade featuring Macy's. Since then he has enjoyed walking on stilts for many events at Universal Studios Orlando, walking stilts for other third party companies, and singing with entertainment central productions in a variety of shows. Before moving to Orlando Brandon working the the Chicago area as a performer at Six Flags Great america, and as a part time educator at Carmel Catholic High School. Brandon possess his bachelor degree in music education from the Universty of Northern Iowa, and currently uses his teaching skills to maintain a small voice studio.
